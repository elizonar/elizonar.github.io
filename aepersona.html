Mock American Eagle Outfitters Campaign

For my content marketing course, I learned the process of creating and planning content for a specific brand.

I completed a semester-long project to research, analyze, and create a campaign for a retail brand. With my familiarity with American Eagle Outfitters, I chose their brand to research. This involved a few kinds of research, including target audience, age demographics, brand voice, and competitors. I combined all these data points into multiple deliverables, including a target persona, mock social media posts, short and long-form blog content, and a campaign calendar for these deliverables.
